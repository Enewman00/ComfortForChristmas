Who are we?

Comfort for Christmas is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing delivering blankets to seniors in nursing homes. Comfort for Christmas was established in 2019 with delivery to one nursing facility. 2020 saw deliveries to two nursing facilities, and now our goal is every nursing facility in Austin that accepts Medicare or Medicaid. We are DREAMING BIG! We were inspired by our love of senior citizens and three very special friends with a heart of gold.
