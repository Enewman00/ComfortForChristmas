What is Comfort for Christmas?

A non profit mission driven organization delivering brand new blankets to older adults in long term care facilities. They are wrapped and labeled “you are loved”. Started by Emily York, Registered Nurse and older adult advocate and inspired by three remarkable women Alisa Barnes, Gayle Morris and Erin Newman.

Comfort for Christmas will expand in 2023 to reach multiple cities!

Store Blankets

Do you have an extra closet? An attic? anywhere NOT dank and smelly. We only provide brand new, nice smelling blankets to seniors. They must not be stored in garage or outside.
